Pound Cake

Description

This delicious buttery poundcake is a family recipe that has been passed down though three generations.
If you are looking for something a little more you can also add fresh blueberries or a flavored glaze.

Ingredients

  • 3c Cake flour
  • 3c Sugar
  • 1/4 tps. Baking Soda
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1c Butter
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 tps. Vanilla
  • 8oz Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°
  2. Cream Sugar and butter
  3. Add eggs one at a time
  4. Combined Sour Cream and Baking Soda
  5. Add salt to flour
  6. Alternate flour and Sour cream mixing it with butter mixture
  7. Add Vanilla
  8. Bake for 1 to 1 hour and 15 minutes
